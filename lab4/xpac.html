Chameleon

Chameleons are a top tier reptile you can't deny that. Chameleons are a very likeable reptile because they're so cool. They have funky eyeballs, a crazy tongue, and they change color. Doesn't get cooler than that.

Crested Gecko

Crested geckos are another one of my favorite reptiles. They have cool looking eyes and weird toes. One of the reptiles I always like to look at when I go to the pet store.
