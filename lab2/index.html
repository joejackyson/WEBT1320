Team Fortress 2 Website

One of my favorite games of all time

My favorite classes to play are Scout and Sniper

The game is an FPS with 9 classes where you have to complete an objective to win

Team Fortress 2 is one of my favorite games. I've played it since 2012, and with the new content added in updates it has remained one of my most played games. In the 9 years I've had the game I have played it for about 4,000 hours. My favorite thing about TF2 is all of the depth in each character. All 9 Classes have completely unique aspects, playstyles, and weapons.

At one point, I even played the game competetively. Competetive matches of TF2 are played in 6v6 format. I competed in a free league with some friends and played 3 seasons as scout. Our team never won first place in any division, but we did move up in divisions for my last season. Even though we didn't come first, I did receive an in-game badge for playing.
