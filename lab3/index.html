Leopard geckos are very cool

I have two leopard geckos of my own. One is named Rodney and the other is named Peach. In general, I find reptiles to be very cool animals.

I have had my leopard geckos for around a year. They've both grown to be very large. They are very easy to handle, and willingly climb into my hand for warmth.

What I feed them

  • Crickets
  • Mealworms
  • Vitaworms
  • Calciworms
