Welcome to Valheim

Valheim is a survival game released in February 2021. Its viking art style and theme sets it apart from other games in the genre. With smooth PvE combat, nice progression systems, and advanced building systems, the game sets itself apart from competitors and has blown up since its release.

Valheim has sold more than 5 million copies so far. With their dev team of only 5 people, they plan on rolling out updates to the game as the year goes on. The game was made over the course of 3 years by a team of 5 veterans of the gaming industry. On release, the game already has hundreds of hours of content.

What do you do in Valheim?

In Valheim, you start out in a meadows biome in the middle of a sacrificial alter. Odin's raven explains to you that you must defeat Odin's enemies in this realm, with each boss residing in a different biome. Your first goal should be to make a base and begin gathering resources. Once you are strong enough, you can take on the boss of the starting biome. Beating one boss unlocks the next biome, and each biome has new resources to use to make yourself stronger.
